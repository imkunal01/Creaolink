Features

Everything a modern
delivery team needs

Built for the gap between Notion's chaos and Jira's rigidity — CreaoLink fits the way creative teams actually work.

⚡
Role-based dashboards
Clients see what they need to approve. Freelancers see what they need to deliver. No more information overload.
🔁
Version-safe collaboration
Push new versions without losing previous context. Every file keeps a timestamped trail with linked feedback threads.
✅
Feedback that resolves
Comments become trackable tasks. Each piece of feedback has an owner, a due date, and a clear resolved state.
📋
Structured project rooms
Spin up scoped workspaces with goals, delivery timelines, and stakeholder assignments in under 2 minutes.
📊
Progress dashboards
Track velocity, closure rates, and revision loops across projects. Spot bottlenecks before they become delays.
🔗
Approval checkpoints
Build formal review stages into every project. Clients approve, freelancers proceed — no ambiguity about what's next.
How it works

Three steps to zero
workflow chaos

A simple operating rhythm that works for agencies, studios, and freelancer pods alike.

Step 01
Create a project room
Define scope, goals, and delivery timelines. Assign clients and freelancers with role-specific access from the start.
Step 02
Collaborate in context
Upload versions, leave feedback directly on deliverables, and keep all communication tied to the exact thing it's about.
Step 03
Close the loop
Convert feedback to tasks, get sign-off through structured approvals, and ship with a full version history intact.
